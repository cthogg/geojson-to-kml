49°11′37.72″ N , 16°34′55.9″ E Villa of the Eisler brothers with a garden, house for two bachelors. Functionalist family house of the interwar period; the work of the prominent Brno architect Otta Eisler, who built it for himself and his brother. The building is integrated into a garden with a number of valuable trees. (originally an orchard)

Note: The memorial catalog lists only one descriptive number, but two indicative numbers - indicated what. 10a belongs to building No. 935.
Listed as a monument since January 31, 1978.
